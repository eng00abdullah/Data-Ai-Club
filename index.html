About the Club

Data & Ai Club is a student-led community at Innovation University that brings together students who are passionate about data, AI, and modern technology.

Our mission is to empower students with practical skills in data analysis, machine learning, and AI through workshops, study groups, and collaborative projects aligned with the vision of Innovation University to shape future-ready leaders.
